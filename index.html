Building Independence, One Step at a Time

Adaptalyfe is specifically designed for teens and adults with autism, ADHD, intellectual disabilities, and other neurodevelopmental conditions who are working toward greater independence in their daily lives.

Visual Task Guidance

Break down complex tasks into manageable steps with visual instructions and progress tracking tailored to your learning style.

Emotional Wellness

Track your mood daily with supportive insights and personalized coping strategies to maintain mental health.

Safety & Emergency

One-tap access to emergency contacts and crisis resources, giving families peace of mind while promoting independence.

AdaptAI Assistant

Get personalized support and encouragement from our AI assistant trained specifically for neurodevelopmental needs.

Medical Management

Organize medications, track symptoms, and manage healthcare information with HIPAA-compliant security.

Family Collaboration

Share progress with caregivers while maintaining privacy and control over your personal information.

Accessibility First

Every feature is designed with accessibility in mind, including:

  • Full screen reader compatibility (VoiceOver, TalkBack)
  • High contrast mode and large text options
  • Voice commands and audio feedback
  • Simple, clear navigation designed for cognitive accessibility
  • Customizable interface based on individual needs
